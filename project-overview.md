# AgriTrust AI

## Overview

AgriTrust AI is a blockchain and AI-powered agricultural traceability platform
built for Africa. It enables farmers, distributors, buyers, and regulators to
track agricultural products from farm to consumer through immutable digital
records, AI-powered insights, and multilingual accessibility — breaking down
language barriers and restoring trust across the supply chain.

## Goals

1. Eliminate counterfeit agricultural products through blockchain-verified Digital Twins and Product Passports
2. Provide end-to-end supply chain transparency from farm registration to consumer delivery
3. Make agricultural information accessible to everyone regardless of language through AI-powered multilingual support (Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, English)
4. Enable seamless payments using stablecoins (USD, cNGN) between supply chain stakeholders
5. Build Africa's most trusted agricultural traceability infrastructure

## Core User Flow

1. Farmer registers their farm and logs a harvest
2. Products are registered and a Digital Twin is created on the blockchain
3. A QR-coded Product Passport is generated for the product
4. Distributor picks up the shipment and updates tracking events in real time
5. Buyer scans the QR code to verify product authenticity and view full history
6. Payment is settled on-chain via the wallet module
7. Regulators can audit the full trail at any time

## Features

### Farm & Harvest Management
- Farm registration with location and metadata
- Harvest registration and batch creation
- Product lifecycle tracking

### Digital Twin & Product Passport
- Blockchain-anchored Digital Identity for every product
- QR code generation and scanning for instant verification
- Full product timeline — from harvest to delivery
- Trust Score and fraud indicators

### Supply Chain Tracking
- Shipment creation and real-time status updates
- Warehouse management and GPS tracking
- Arrival notifications

### Wallet & Payments
- Stablecoin payments (USD, cNGN)
- Transaction history and payment receipts

### AI Platform
- Voice assistant with Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech
- Multilingual translation (Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, English)
- Market and buyer recommendations
- Fraud detection and anomaly detection
- Trust Score engine

### Access Control
- Role-based access: Admin, Farmer, Buyer, Transporter, Regulator

## Scope

### In Scope
- Farm, harvest, and product registration
- Digital Twin creation and blockchain anchoring
- QR-based Product Passport and verification
- Supply chain shipment tracking
- On-chain wallet and payments (USD, cNGN stablecoins)
- AI voice assistant and multilingual support
- Fraud detection and Trust Score
- Analytics dashboard
- Regulatory audit trail

### Out of Scope
- Physical logistics / last-mile delivery operations
- Marketplace (planned for future release)
- Mobile native app (web-first for now)

## Success Criteria

1. A farmer can register a farm, log a harvest, and generate a verifiable Product Passport end to end
2. A buyer can scan a QR code and view the complete, tamper-proof product history
3. A shipment update made by a distributor is reflected on-chain within seconds
4. An AI assistant responds accurately in at least one African language (Yoruba, Hausa, or Igbo)
5. A payment can be initiated and settled between two stakeholders using cNGN or USD stablecoin
